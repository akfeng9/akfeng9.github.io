Summary

Everything Everywhere All at Once is a brilliant film that encapsulates the importance of choosing love and human connection. It also introduces the complex dynamics within immigrant Asian families, balancing familial obligation and independence.

Characters

There are four main actors in the story:

  1. Evelyn Wang, the main protagonast
  2. Waymond Wang, husband of Evelyn Wang
  3. Joy/Jobu Tupaki, the main antagonist
  4. Deirdre Beaubeirdre, another antagonist

Dimensions

Although Evelyn and Joy both travel through several universes, there are many that the movie focuses on:

  • the "Alpha-Verse"
  • Movie Star Universe
  • Hotdog Finger Universe
  • Rock Universe
